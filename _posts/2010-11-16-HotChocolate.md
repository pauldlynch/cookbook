Heat 3 cups milk till bubbling.

Combine and add:

- 4 tbs unsweetened cocoa
- 1/4 c sugar, 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/8 tsp each cloves & salt, 1 Tsp each instant coffee & Vanilla

Beat Till frothy.
Pour into Mugs.
Dust With Cinnamon.
