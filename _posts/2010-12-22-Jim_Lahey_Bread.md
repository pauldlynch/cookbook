- 400g flour (3 cups)
- 8g / 1.25 tsp salt
- 1g / 0.25 tsp yeast
- 300g water (1 1/3 cups)

Mix, rise 12-18 hrs.  Shape, prove 1-2 hrs. Preheat oven & casserole 30 mins. 475F 30 mins covered, + 15-30 mins uncovered.
